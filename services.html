Programs & Activities

At Thank You Challenge, we are committed to fostering growth, gratitude, and connection in our community. Below are some of our core programs and activities, each designed to inspire, support, and empower you on your journey.

Conferences & Seminars

We hold annual or bi-annual conferences and seminars to inspire, empower, and equip believers for effective Christian living and ministry. These gatherings promote spiritual renewal, community, and leadership development, with dynamic speakers, leaders, and worship teams providing practical tools and spiritual insights for personal growth.

Community Visits

Visiting orphanages and widows is a meaningful way to demonstrate practical gratitude. By spending time with those who may feel forgotten or alone, we are able to share kindness and compassion in a tangible way. These visits not only provide much-needed encouragement and support to the residents, but they also remind us to appreciate the blessings in our own lives. Bringing gifts, sharing meals, or simply engaging in heartfelt conversations can make a significant difference, spreading joy and hope to people who need it most.
