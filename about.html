About Thank You Challenge

The Thank You Challenge is dedicated to fostering a Kingdom culture among Christians, encouraging them to live a lifestyle of continual thanksgiving to God. We are committed to teaching and raising up believers who not only cultivate this attitude of gratitude but also remain passionately consistent in expressing thanks to God, regardless of the difficulties or challenges they may face in life. Through this challenge, we aim to cultivate resilient and grateful hearts within the Christian community.

Our Objectives

  • Promote a culture of thanksgiving among Christians.
  • Raise awareness about gratitude.
  • Use thanksgiving as a Kingdom weapon to overcome challenges.

Future Goals

We plan to host conferences, produce YouTube content, provide T-shirts with our colours, and support local communities via visits to orphanages and widows.
