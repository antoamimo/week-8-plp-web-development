Welcome to Thank You Challenge (TYC)

We are dedicated to building a Kingdom culture where Africans live a lifestyle of thanksgiving, service, and generosity. TYC is more than an initiative—it's a movement that equips, empowers, and inspires believers to embody gratitude daily.

Vision

To establish a Kingdom culture where Christians live a lifestyle of thanksgiving unto God.

Mission

Inspire and equip believers through teachings, community outreach, and acts of gratitude that impact generations.
